Light Board
The lightboard is an onboard LED signalling system designed for the Shell Eco-marathon vehicle to indicate key operational states, including braking. When the brake is engaged, the system activates a visible brake light to notify surrounding vehicles and race officials that the car is decelerating or stopping. This improves on-track safety and ensures compliance with competition visibility requirements.
